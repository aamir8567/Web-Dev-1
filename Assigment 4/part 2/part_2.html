Sematic tags are very important for every document, because many people use screen readers to surf the web, and the screen readers uses the sematic tags assigned to content to process the command. Another reason why sematic tags are important is to boost your page's rankings, because search engines also use the sematic tags to determine the type of content your page has.

You might think that sematic tags are just for the end user, and does nothing to you as a coder, but you would be wrong, because sematic tags makes your code more organized and consistent in terms of css designs. Sematic tags are also considered to be a best practice in the coding community of creating webpages.
